Another example is the flow model, inspired by fluid dynamics \cite{...}. The Boltzmann machine, which has been used to solve the Ising model \cite{carleo2015}, is inspired by statistical physics. Several of these methods are applied and investigated in my projects. On this webpage I will publish selected projects that I have been working on, either alone or in collaboration with others.
